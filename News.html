Providing School Uniforms for Underprivileged Children

Through the skills and dedication of our team, along with the generosity of donors, we have been able to provide school uniforms to underprivileged primary and secondary school children in Malawi. This initiative ensures that students from disadvantaged backgrounds are not held back by the lack of uniforms, allowing them to attend school with confidence and focus on their education.

Sanitation and Water for All: A Vital Global Initiative

Water is a basic human right, yet millions still struggle to access it. The Sanitation and Water for All global partnership, hosted by UNICEF, works to provide safe, clean water and sanitation to those in need. This initiative emphasizes the importance of water in achieving dignity, peace, and survival, as shared by CEO Catarina de Albuquerque during her recent speech in Lisbon.

Hosted By: UNICEF

Keynote Speaker: Catarina de Albuquerque
